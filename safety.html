🧠 Tourist Safety Tips

📱 Save local emergency numbers before you travel to remote areas.
📍 Always share your itinerary with a family member or friend.
🌧️ Check weather alerts before heading to hill areas — landslides are common during monsoon.
🪪 Carry a valid ID and Inner Line Permit (ILP) if required for your destination.
💊 Carry basic medicines — pharmacies can be scarce in remote areas.
🔋 Keep your phone charged and carry a power bank on treks.
